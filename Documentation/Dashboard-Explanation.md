# Dashboard Architecture & Explanation

## Dashboard Types

### Executive Dashboard – “What Is Happening”
Designed for leadership to quickly assess overall business performance.

#### Key Features
- KPI cards for revenue, profit, orders, customers
- Sales by region
- Top countries, industries, and products

#### Design Philosophy
- Minimal interaction
- High clarity
- Low cognitive load

---

### Analytics Dashboard – “Why It Is Happening”
Built for analysts and product managers to investigate performance drivers.

#### Key Analyses
- Sales vs Profit by Product
- Revenue per Customer by Product
- Regional efficiency comparison
- Discount impact analysis

#### Design Philosophy
- Comparative visuals
- Root-cause analysis
- Exploratory interaction

---

## Key Visual Explanations

### Sales by Region
Shows revenue contribution across Americas, EMEA, and APJ.

### Sales vs Profit by Product
Highlights products with high revenue but low profitability.

### Revenue per Customer
Identifies products delivering highest customer value.

### Discount per Product
Evaluates discounting patterns and potential margin erosion.
