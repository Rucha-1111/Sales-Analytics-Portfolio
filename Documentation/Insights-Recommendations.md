# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Findings

### Revenue Concentration Risk
A small subset of products drives most revenue and profit, creating portfolio dependency risk.

Several products generate strong sales but weak profit due to discounting or cost structure issues.

### Regional Revenue Imbalance
Despite equal customer distribution, EMEA generates significantly higher revenue per customer than APJ.

### Discount Strategy Weakness
Inconsistent discounting practices risk long-term margin erosion.

### Industry Specialization Success
Finance sector dominance validates vertical-specific product strategy.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Strengthen profitability-focused product management
2. Implement discount governance policies
3. Expand revenue per customer in APJ through upselling
4. Replicate Finance vertical success in other industries
5. Invest in customer success to improve repeat purchasing
