# Metrics & Dimensions Definitions

## Key Metrics

### Total Sales
- **Definition:** Sum of all revenue from completed transactions
- **Business Value:** Measures top-line growth
- **Dashboard Value:** $2.30M

### Total Profit
- **Definition:** Revenue minus associated costs
- **Business Value:** Indicates financial sustainability
- **Dashboard Value:** $286.40K

### Total Orders
- **Definition:** Count of completed sales transactions
- **Business Value:** Measures transaction volume
- **Dashboard Value:** 5,009

### Total Customers
- **Definition:** Unique customer accounts
- **Business Value:** Indicates market penetration
- **Dashboard Value:** 9,994

### Average Order Value (AOV)
- **Formula:** Total Sales รท Total Orders
- **Business Value:** Indicates deal size
- **Dashboard Value:** $458.61

### Revenue per Customer
- **Formula:** Total Sales รท Total Customers
- **Business Value:** Measures customer value
- **Dashboard Value:** $23.20K

### Profit Margin
- **Formula:** (Profit รท Sales) ร 100
- **Business Value:** Measures pricing and cost efficiency
- **Dashboard Value:** 12.45%
